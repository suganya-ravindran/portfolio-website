The Technical Evolution

From IT Support & Operations to Cloud Architecture.

Full Circle

My career began in the trenches of IT Support at IBM. As a Technical Support Executive, I spent two years troubleshooting network security and resolving system crashes, building a "fix-it-first" mindset that still drives me today.

I then took a strategic pivot to Ernst & Young as a Advanced Tax Analyst to understand the business side of global operations. While managing data governance and compliance for mobile employees, I worked closely with systems like SAP. It was there, watching how technology underpinned complex business workflows, that I realized I missed the technical field.

I transitioned back into tech operations at Kaleyra, managing API infrastructure, and am now fully committed to Cloud Architecture. I combine the troubleshooting skills from IBM with the rigorous compliance discipline from EY to build secure, business-ready cloud solutions.
